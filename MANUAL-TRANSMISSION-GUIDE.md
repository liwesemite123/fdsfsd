## RPM Gauge

### Understanding RPM:
- **1000-4000 RPM** - Low (green zone)
- **4000-7000 RPM** - Optimal (magenta)
- **7000-8000 RPM** - High (shift recommended)
- **8000+ RPM** - Redline! (red, over-revving)

### Color Coding:
- **Magenta (#ff00ff)** - Normal operation
- **Red (#ff0000)** - Over 90% redline, shift NOW!

## Cooldown Period

**300ms shift cooldown** - Cannot shift faster than 0.3 seconds
- Prevents accidental double-shifts
- Realistic transmission behavior
- Visual feedback during cooldown

## Troubleshooting

**Gear not changing?**
- Check if 300ms has passed since last shift
- Ensure race is in progress
- Verify you're not already in min/max gear

**Car not moving?**
- Check if you're in Neutral (N)
- Shift to 1st gear to start moving

**Speed seems limited?**
- Shift to higher gear
- Check if over-revving (red RPM)
- Ensure proper gear for current speed

**RPM showing wrong?**
- RPM calculated based on speed and gear
- Higher gear = lower RPM at same speed
- Lower gear = higher RPM at same speed
